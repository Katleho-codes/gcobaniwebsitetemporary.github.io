A little about me...

Gcobani Nyathela also known as "Bumbinox Films" is a South African videographer born in Eastern Cape, South Africa.
A growing Digital Creating Videographer focused mainly on music videos, short films and documentaries.
Worked with the likes of CrownedYoung , Kenny Black , Bhut'Legend & Brian .
Currently on more projects that are soon to be released.
Appeared on the 2018 edition of 'Being magazine', page 16 of which Siviwe Lutseke was on the cover of.
Appeared on the "The Juice Up" podcast on Spotify and was the first person to reach 100+ listeners on the podcasts, bearing Unathi Mandela
